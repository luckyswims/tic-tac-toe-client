Lucky's Tic-Tac-Toe

Tic-Tac-Toe is a simple game in which players take turns claiming squares on a three by three grid. Players claim a square by marking it with either an X or an O. A player wins by claiming three squares in a row, column, or diagonal.

To start playing, click the "Register" button in the navigation bar above. Once you have registerd click the "Login" button to log in.

If you want to play a game, click on the Game button in the navigation bar to navigate to the Game screen.
If you want to play against another person on the same computer click New Local Game.
If you want to play against a computer opponent:

  • click New Easy Game to play against an easy opponent, or
  • click New Medium Game to play against a more difficult opponent, or
  • click New Impossible Game to play against a truly perfect opponent

After you have played some games you can check out some statistics by clicking Statistics.
